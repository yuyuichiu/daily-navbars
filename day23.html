Dice Game

Roll five dice and see how much score you get!

Rules are as follow:

  • Three 1: 1000pt
  • Three 2: 200pt
  • Three 3: 300pt
  • Three 4: 400pt
  • Three 5: 500pt
  • Three 6: 600pt
  • Single 1: 100pt
  • Single 5: 50pt
  • Each dice can only be counted in one roll, and each roll can be only triggered once
